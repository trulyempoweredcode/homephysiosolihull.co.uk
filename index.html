Who I Help

Is Home Visit Physiotherapy Right for You?

Home physiotherapy allows me to assess how you move in your real-life environment and tailor treatment to your needs. It is particularly suited to people who:

  • Are recovering from surgery
  • Need elderly rehabilitation
  • Are losing mobility or confidence
  • Have reduced mobility or balance concerns
  • Have a neurological condition (stroke, Parkinson's, MS)
  • Feel uncomfortable travelling to appointments
  • Prefer flexible, personalised care at home
  • Have recently been discharged from hospital
What I Treat

Conditions I Commonly Help With

Every treatment plan is personalised and designed to help you or your loved one regain confidence and independence.

Post-Operative Rehabilitation

Structured recovery after orthopaedic, abdominal or other surgery — restoring strength, range of movement and confidence in everyday tasks.

Falls Prevention & Balance

Practical strength, balance and confidence work to reduce falls risk — assessed and progressed within your own home.

Parkinson's, MS & Stroke

Specialist neurological physiotherapy at your own pace — improving balance, coordination and day-to-day movement.

Arthritis Pain & Stiffness

Hands-on treatment, gentle exercise and joint-protection advice to ease pain and keep you moving comfortably.

Back, Neck & Joint Pain

Assessment and targeted treatment for persistent back, neck and joint pain — with clear guidance you can use between sessions.

General Mobility Concerns

Calm, structured rehabilitation focused on strength, balance and safe independence within the home.

For Families

Specialist Support for Older Adults & Families

If you are arranging physiotherapy for a parent or loved one, I understand how important it is to find someone experienced, patient and trustworthy. I take time to explain findings clearly, answer questions and provide reassurance throughout treatment.

Home physiotherapy is particularly helpful for older adults who have recently been discharged from hospital, experienced a fall or feel unsteady, are losing confidence with mobility, or need support managing arthritis or joint pain.

If you would like to discuss your parent's situation before booking, I am happy to speak with you first.

Why Choose Home Visits

Treatment Where You Feel Most Comfortable

For many people, home visits feel more relaxed, more personal and more practical.

I come to you. No clinic waiting rooms, no awkward car journeys when you're already in pain.
