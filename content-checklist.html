Content Checklist

A short list of items to supply before the site goes live. Please send anything you have to contact@homephysiosolihull.co.uk — and tell me which items you'd like to skip.

Most Important

Testimonials & Social Proof

The site has no testimonials section yet. We'd like to add 3–5 short patient quotes for the homepage and a couple of pages — these are the single biggest trust boost we can add.

  • 3–5 short patient testimonials (1–3 sentences each). First name + last initial only is fine.
  • For each: the condition treated (e.g. “hip pain”, “post-stroke rehab”) and the patient's area if known (e.g. Solihull, Shirley).
  • Written consent from each patient that the quote can be displayed publicly on the website.
  • A Google Business Profile / Google Reviews link if you have one — we can wire this in.
Branding

Logo & Photography

  • A dedicated logo for "Home Physio Solihull". (Currently the nav uses a text brand. The Shirley Physiotherapy PNG in the build folder is for a different brand and isn't being used.)
  • If you'd prefer to use a real personal photo of you in place of the AI-generated portrait on the homepage and About page, please supply a high-resolution image.
  • Optional: any clinic-style photos of you assessing a patient, or treating in a home setting — fully consented.
Business Details

Confirm or Add

  • Confirm the trading address — currently we've listed "Solihull, West Midlands" (no street/postcode) for the footer and schema. Let me know if you'd like a registered business address added.
  • Confirm the Stripe / payment processor wording you'd like on the Fees page (currently shown as "card, bank transfer or cash").
  • Any social media handles you'd like in the footer (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn).
  • Working hours / contact hours, if you want them displayed on the Contact page.
Trust & Credentials

Optional Additions

We've only displayed credentials supplied in your content (HCPC, CSP, BSc Hons, PG Dip Health Studies, PG Dip Injection Therapy, 16+ years). Let us know if you'd like to add any of the following.

  • Public liability and professional indemnity insurance details (provider + cover amount).
  • Enhanced DBS check confirmation if you'd like to display it.
  • Any additional CPD courses you'd like featured (acupuncture body, neuro-rehab certificates etc.).
Technical

  • The contact form action is set to https://editmy.site/api/form/{site_id}. Once we register the site in the editor portal we'll replace {site_id} with the real numeric ID.
  • SMTP details for sending form submissions to your inbox (provider + outgoing server + username — we configure password securely on the server).
