About Greg

Hello, I'm Greg Davies

An experienced physiotherapist providing professional home visit physiotherapy across Solihull, Birmingham and surrounding areas.

My Background

Sixteen years' experience, brought into your home

I've worked with a wide range of patients — from those recovering after surgery to individuals managing long-term joint pain or reduced mobility. My focus is simple: to provide high-quality, personalised physiotherapy for seniors and adults in the comfort of your own home.

For many older adults, travelling to a clinic adds stress and difficulty to an already challenging time. Home visits remove that barrier — and they let me see how you actually move through your day, in the rooms where it matters.

Get in Touch
My Approach

Restoring Movement, Confidence and Independence

I understand that pain, reduced mobility, recent injury or having a fall can significantly affect confidence, independence and your quality of life. My aim is not only to reduce pain, but to help you live more freely.

01

Restore Movement

Hands-on treatment combined with targeted exercise to improve range of motion, ease stiffness and help you move more comfortably day to day.

02

Improve Strength & Balance

Practical strength and balance work, progressed at your pace, designed to make everyday movement feel safer and more confident.

03

Support Safe Independence

Treatment built around your home — stairs, transfers, daily routines — so what you practise in sessions translates directly into real life.

04

Build Long-Term Resilience

Clear, achievable plans that you can keep using once treatment ends, so the gains you make are sustained rather than slipping back.

Credentials

Qualifications & Professional Registration

I am fully qualified and professionally registered. These registrations ensure I meet strict professional standards, maintain ongoing professional development, and practise safely and ethically.

BSc (Hons) Physiotherapy

Bachelor's degree in Physiotherapy — the foundational clinical qualification for practice in the United Kingdom.

PG Diploma Health Studies

Postgraduate Diploma in Health Studies — advanced study in clinical practice and patient care.

PG Diploma Injection Therapy

Postgraduate Diploma in Injection Therapy — qualified to administer joint injections where clinically appropriate.

HCPC Registered

Registered with the Health and Care Professions Council — the legally required regulator for physiotherapists in the UK.

CSP Member

Member of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy — the professional, educational and trade union body for chartered physiotherapists.

16+ Years' Experience

In musculoskeletal and rehabilitation physiotherapy across NHS and private settings.

Why I Do Home Visits

Treatment That Fits Your Real Life

Home physiotherapy allows me to assess and treat patients within their real-life environment — leading to better consistency, confidence and outcomes.

Address Stairs, Transfers & Daily Movement

I can see the actual obstacles you navigate every day, and build practical solutions around them.

Practical Rehabilitation Tailored to the Home

Exercises and techniques you can keep using in your own space, with the chairs, beds and surfaces you actually use.

Reduce Travel-Related Stress

No clinic commute, no parking, no waiting room. Especially important when pain or recovery already makes movement difficult.

Truly One-to-One Focused Care

Your full appointment time is yours — no shared spaces, no interruptions. Just unhurried, focused treatment.
