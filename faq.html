Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know about home visit physiotherapy with me. If your question isn't covered here, feel free to get in touch.

I provide home physiotherapy across Shirley, Solihull, Hall Green, Wythall, Hollywood, Earlswood, Knowle, Dorridge, Kings Norton, Kings Heath, Moseley, Longbridge and Birmingham. If you're unsure whether I cover your location, please get in touch — I'm always happy to help where I can.

I treat a wide range of conditions, including muscle and joint pain, mobility and balance problems, rehabilitation after surgery, and neurological conditions such as stroke, Parkinson's disease and multiple sclerosis. If you're unsure whether physiotherapy is right for you, feel free to ask — I'll guide you honestly.

Yes — all my appointments are carried out in the comfort of your own home. This allows treatment to be more personalised and relevant to your everyday environment, without the need to travel.

Your first session includes a thorough assessment, where I discuss your symptoms and medical history, understand your goals and concerns, assess movement, strength and function, and begin treatment with advice or exercises. I take a calm, supportive approach and ensure you feel comfortable throughout.

Initial appointments typically last around 45–60 minutes. Follow-up sessions are around 30–45 minutes, depending on your needs.
