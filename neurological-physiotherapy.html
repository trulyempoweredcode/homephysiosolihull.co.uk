Neurological Physiotherapy

Calm, Specialist Support at Your Own Pace

Living with a neurological condition can feel overwhelming — not just physically, but emotionally too. I'm here to support you gently, in the comfort of your own home.

Personalised Care

Rebuilding Confidence, Movement and Independence

Everyday tasks that were once simple may become more difficult, and it's natural to feel uncertain about what comes next. I provide personalised, one-to-one treatment in the comfort of your own home — helping you rebuild confidence, improve movement, and regain as much independence as possible.

There is no rush — just steady, meaningful progress at a pace that works for you.

Talk to Greg
How Physiotherapy Can Help

Treatment Tailored to You

Every person's experience is different, which is why treatment is always tailored to you. Neurological physiotherapy can help with:

  • Improving balance and reducing the risk of falls
  • Regaining strength and coordination
  • Making walking and everyday movement easier
  • Reducing stiffness, tightness or muscle weakness
  • Supporting recovery after a stroke
  • Managing long-term conditions like Parkinson's or MS
  • Building confidence in day-to-day activities
  • Feeling safer and more in control
What to Expect

Relaxed, Supportive Sessions Centred on You

Appointments are calm and unhurried. I will:

Carry Out a Gentle Assessment

A thorough but gentle assessment of your strength, balance, mobility and how your condition affects your daily life.

Listen Carefully

I take time to understand your concerns and priorities — what matters most to you, and what you want to be able to do again.

Create a Clear Plan Together

A clear, achievable plan with simple, effective exercises that fit into your day and progress steadily over time.

Reassure You and Your Family

Ongoing support, encouragement and clear communication so you and your loved ones feel informed and confident throughout.

A Caring Approach at Home

Practising Where Real Life Happens

Receiving physiotherapy at home allows you to practise movements in your own environment, making improvements more practical and relevant to daily life. It also removes the stress of travelling to appointments.

My aim is simple: to help you feel more confident, more capable, and more supported every step of the way.
