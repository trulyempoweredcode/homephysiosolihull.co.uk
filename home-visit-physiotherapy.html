Home Visit Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy in the Comfort of Your Own Home

Gentle, personalised care from an experienced physiotherapist — helping you move more comfortably and confidently in a familiar environment where you feel at ease.

Specialist Home Visits

One-to-One Physiotherapy for Seniors and Adults

I understand that pain, injury or reduced mobility can make even simple tasks feel difficult. My home physiotherapy service is designed to bring expert care to you — with no clinic travel, no waiting rooms, and no rush.

Home visits are my primary service. They let me see how you actually move through your day, in the spaces where it matters — making treatment more practical, more relevant, and far easier to apply between sessions.

Book a Home Visit
What I Treat at Home

Conditions Home Physiotherapy Can Help With

My home visit physiotherapy service covers a wide range of needs for older adults and seniors.

  • Post-operative rehabilitation
  • Falls prevention and balance training
  • Mobility support for older adults
  • Strength and conditioning rehabilitation
  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Parkinson's disease
  • Stroke rehabilitation
  • Multiple sclerosis (MS)
  • General elderly rehabilitation
What to Expect

A Calm, Unhurried Visit Built Around You

During your home visit, I take the time to truly understand your situation. Everything is adapted to your goals, your pace, and your comfort.

1
Step One

Thorough Assessment

A careful, unhurried assessment of your condition, history, and how it affects your day-to-day life. I listen first, then assess movement, strength and function.

2
Step Two

Clear Plan & Hands-On Treatment

Simple explanations of what's causing your symptoms, hands-on treatment where appropriate, and a plan tailored to your goals.

3
Step Three

Guided Exercise & Advice

Gentle, guided exercises tailored to your ability, plus practical advice to help you feel more confident in your movement day-to-day.

Who This Is For

Is Home Visit Physiotherapy Right for You?

This service is particularly helpful if you:

  • Are experiencing ongoing pain or stiffness
  • Are losing mobility or confidence on your feet
  • Have a neurological condition
  • Are recovering from surgery or injury
  • Feel less steady or confident on your feet
  • Prefer treatment in a private, familiar setting
  • Find travelling to a clinic difficult
  • Are arranging care for a parent or loved one
