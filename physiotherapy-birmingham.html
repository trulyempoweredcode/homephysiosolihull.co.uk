One-to-One Care at Home

Specialist Physiotherapy Across Birmingham

Home physiotherapy appointments are available across Birmingham, including support for mobility concerns, falls prevention and recovery following surgery. With over 16 years of clinical experience, I work with patients across the Birmingham area to rebuild strength, balance and confidence — all without the stress of travelling to a clinic.

I'm Greg Davies — an HCPC registered physiotherapist with over 16 years of clinical experience. I provide one-to-one, personalised treatment in the comfort of your own home, removing the stress of travelling to a clinic so you can focus on recovery.

What I Treat in Birmingham

Conditions I Commonly Help With

Every treatment plan is personalised. Common reasons Birmingham residents get in touch include:

  • Post-operative rehabilitation
  • Falls prevention and balance issues
  • Parkinson's, MS & stroke rehabilitation
  • General mobility concerns
  • Arthritis pain and stiffness
  • Back, neck and joint pain
How It Works

What to Expect From a Home Visit

No clinic, no rush. Just three steady steps from first call to feeling better.

1
Step One
