Physiotherapy after a stroke

Caring Physiotherapy for You or a Loved One

A stroke can change everyday life in an instant, affecting movement, balance, strength, confidence, speech, and independence. Recovery can feel overwhelming for both the individual and their family, but with the right support, meaningful progress is possible.

I provide compassionate home physiotherapy for people recovering from stroke in the comfort and familiarity of their own home. My aim is to help you or your loved one regain confidence, improve mobility, and maximise independence at a pace that feels safe and achievable.

Receiving physiotherapy at home can often feel less stressful and more practical than attending busy clinics. Therapy is tailored to real-life daily activities within your own environment, helping recovery become more meaningful and functional.

How Physiotherapy Can Help After a Stroke

Every stroke affects people differently, which is why treatment is always personalised to individual needs and goals. Physiotherapy may help with:

  • Improving walking and mobility
  • Increasing strength and balance
  • Reducing falls risk
  • Improving transfers such as getting in and out of bed or chairs
  • Rehabilitation following hospital discharge
  • Improving confidence and independence
  • Reducing stiffness and muscle tightness
  • Supporting safe use of walking aids
  • Fatigue management and pacing
  • Supporting family members and carers

I understand that stroke recovery can be emotional as well as physical. Sessions are delivered with patience, encouragement, and understanding to help people feel supported throughout their rehabilitation journey.

Home Visits Designed Around You

Home physiotherapy allows rehabilitation to take place where it matters most — in your everyday environment. Treatment can focus on practical tasks such as:

  • Walking safely around the home
  • Managing stairs
  • Improving balance indoors and outdoors
  • Transfers into bed, chairs, or cars
  • Confidence with daily activities
  • Building strength and endurance safely

Family members are always welcome to be involved in sessions if desired, helping everyone feel reassured and supported.

Gentle, Personalised Rehabilitation

Recovery after stroke takes time, and progress can vary from person to person. My approach is calm, caring, and focused on realistic goals that matter most to you or your loved one.

Whether support is needed after a recent stroke or ongoing rehabilitation is required months or years later, I aim to provide compassionate physiotherapy that helps improve quality of life and confidence at home.
